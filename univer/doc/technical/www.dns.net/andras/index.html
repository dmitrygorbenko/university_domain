Here is some personal background about Andras Salamon (that's me). Although born in Hungary, I am not the Hungarian film-maker András Salamon, nor the Hungarian software developer András Salamon who did postgraduate work at University of Nebraska-Lincoln in constraint databases in 1997/8, nor Dr András Salamon the Hungarian representative of the Zambia National Tourist Board (with all of whom I share my name, and some of whom may coincide with each other).

I used the email address andras@is.co.za from 1994 to 2001, sometimes in the variant form andras@apollo.is.co.za. These addresses are no longer active. While at the University of the Witwatersrand, I used a cs.wits.ac.za email address; this is still active (although the variants including host names concave, titan etc. are not). All these addresses are scattered around the web on technical mailing list archives, USENET postings, and old source code.
